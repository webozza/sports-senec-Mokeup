# Sport Sense — Custom Trophies redesign direction

## Goal

Turn the page from a catalogue index into a **quote-generation landing page**. The visitor should understand the service in five seconds, find their award type, and submit a brief without needing to open several PDFs.

## Current-page review

What is already good:

- The black / orange system is recognisable, sporty and high contrast.
- The five-step process is reassuring and explains proof approval well.
- The content addresses the main trophy groups and has clear phone/email details.

What needs to change:

- There is no hero message, product image or primary action above the fold. "Custom Trophies & Awards" alone does not explain the benefit.
- The page begins with process, then asks people to choose between 16 nearly identical PDF cards. This creates decision fatigue and moves visitors away from Sport Sense.
- The first meaningful action is a phone/email CTA at the bottom, after a very long page. Put **Request a quote** in the header and hero.
- The whole page uses a narrow content width. On desktop it gives a dense, template-like feel despite the strong brand palette.
- "2025 product range" is outdated in 2026. Update catalogue titles every year or remove the year from customer-facing labels.
- There is no social proof (recent work, turnaround expectation, review, number of local projects) and no simple brief form.

## Recommended brand system

Keep the existing identity; do not redesign the logo yet. Refine the usage:

| Role | Colour | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Core dark | `#1F1F21` | Page background, navigation, body text on orange |
| Brand orange | `#FC9402` | Primary CTA, highlighted number, key link |
| Warm white | `#F8F5EF` | Light section background and readable display type |
| Slate | `#343942` | Borders / quiet panels |
| Signal aqua | `#00FCED` | Small optional accent only: labels, selection state, never a second CTA |

Typography: use **Barlow Condensed** (or current Oswald) for compact all-caps headings; use **DM Sans** (or current Figtree) for body text. This keeps the athletic personality but makes paragraphs and forms easier to read.

## Mockup structure

1. Announcement + simple header, with a persistent **Request a quote** CTA.
2. Hero: clear outcome-led message, real trophy image, two actions.
3. Three quick paths: browse by occasion / see process / ask for fast help.
4. Four visual category cards: Sport, Corporate, School & Community, Design from scratch.
5. The five-step process, in an easy mobile stack.
6. High-contrast quote panel followed by a short quote form.

## Build priorities for Shopify

1. Add a `Custom trophy hero` section with editable image, eyebrow, heading, CTA and trust points.
2. Replace the two 8-card PDF grids with category tiles; each tile opens a filtered collection or a small modal with its best catalogue links.
3. Add a 6-field form: name, email, phone, event date, quantity, requirements / logo upload. Send it to the same email inbox.
4. Add a small gallery of 6 real completed Sport Sense jobs, organised by Sport / Corporate / School.
5. Add turnaround guidance only if it is operationally reliable (for example, "quote within 1 business day").
